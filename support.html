✈️ Welcome to Aviation Capital Simulator

Aviation Capital Simulator is a real-time airline management and operational simulation platform. In ACS, you become the founder, CEO, and Operations Director of your own airline.

Your mission is to build, operate, and expand a profitable aviation company inside a dynamic global aviation world. Every decision matters: fleet acquisition, route planning, maintenance, finance, leasing, airport slots, and live operations.

🏛 ACS OCC Experience

ACS Command Center Experience. You are not only buying aircraft; you are commanding an airline operation through finance, operations, maintenance, scheduling, and strategic control.

💰 Credits & ACS Tokens

ACS uses an economic system based on operational credits and ACS Tokens. Credits are used for airline operations such as aircraft acquisition, leasing, maintenance, payroll, route development, and company growth.

ACS Tokens are part of the premium ACS ecosystem and may be used for future services, strategic features, special events, marketplace activity, and platform expansion.

🎯 Your Objective

Start with one airline. Build your fleet. Open routes. Manage your finances. Control your operations through ACS OCC and grow into a global aviation empire.
